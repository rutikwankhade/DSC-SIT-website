About Us

Developer Student Club SIT, Lonavala is inspired by Google Developers Family.
We started our journey in Feb 2019. We are a group of passionate students who try to engage
fellow student developers through our events, workshops, codelabs and meetups.
The motive is to create a local ecosystem of programmers & developers in and around the Campus.
